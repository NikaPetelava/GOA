Healthy Eating Guide

Welcome to your guide to heathy eating. Below, you'll find tips, food groups, and Iinks to resources to maintaining a balanced diet.

Food Groups

  • Fruits
  • Vegetables
  • Proteins
  • Grains
  • Dairy

Fruits

Fruits are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. They are an essential part of a balanced diet.

Vegetables

Vegetables provide important nutrients like vitamins, minerals, and fiber. Eating a variety of vegetables is crucial for good health.

Proteins

Proteins are the building blocks of the body. They are important for muscle repair and overall body function.

Grains

Grains, especially whole grains, provide essential carbohydrates and fiber. They are a key energy source.

Dairy

Dairy products like milk, cheese, and yogurt are rich in calcium and protein, important for bone health.

Healthy Eating Tips

  • Eat a variety of foods from all food groups.
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains.
  • Include plenty of fruits and vegetables in your diet.
  • Opt for lean proteins like chicken, fish, and legumes.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
